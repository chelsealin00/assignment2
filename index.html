All about facials

What is a facial and why do I need one?

Facials are a great way to unwind and relax and get glowing and healthy skin all at the same time

It involves skincare steps like masks, massage, exfoliating and more, all of which benefit your skin a great deal.

Click on the images to learn more!


Who can get a facial?


Although your skin problems start as early as your teenage; it is recommended (by the skincare experts) that you wait until you are at least 20 to get a facial. Until then you can carry out proper cleansing at home yourself and stick to a good skincare regimen that works for your skin type.

Types of facials?


The basic ones include a hydrating fruit facial, brightening facial, cleansing facial and more, while the more advanced ones like paraffin facials, chemical peel facials, de tan facials and collagen facials are specific to skin problems and age.

Results of a facial?


Apart from doing away with your skin issues, facials leave your skin incredibly soft and plump. You notice a visible glow on your skin and a brighter and radiant complexion.
